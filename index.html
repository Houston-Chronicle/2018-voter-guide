The Houston Chronicle Voter Guide

By Alejandra Matos, Jordan Rubio and Rachael Gleason

We invited candidates running for statewide and federal offices in the 2018 midterm elections to answer questions about key issues facing Texas. The candidates’ unedited responses are presented as submitted to the Houston Chronicle. Some candidates did not respond to our questionnaire.

An asterisk (*) next to a candidate's name indicates the candidate was endorsed by the Houston Chronicle Editorial Board, which is independent from the Houston Chronicle newsroom.
